Fire Shows

Full professional fire performances featuring poi, staff, fans, snakes, hoops, and partner flow. Choreographed shows tailored to your event's theme, atmosphere, and audience.

Fire-free LED flow arts for indoor events, venues with fire restrictions, or mixed LED/fire experiences. Visually stunning, safe, and endlessly creative.
