Our Story

A Life Without
Borders.

We are Jesse and Catherine Hart — soul partners, performers, coaches, and nomadic visionaries from the United States. Six and a half years in. Thirty countries deep. One daughter born on the road. One mission: Unite The World First Hand.

The Journey

Six Years, One Road

2019
Oregon, USA → Mexico
We load a camper truck and drive south out of Oregon. The plan: drive the whole world. What began as a bold dream becomes a living commitment.
2019–2021
Central & South America
Guatemala, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Brazil. Fire shows on beaches. Yoga on volcanoes. Community everywhere.
2021
Prana Born in Ecuador 🌿
Our daughter Prana is born in Ecuador — the first country she'll ever call home. She has never been to the United States. Every country has been her home.
2022–2023
Shipped to Africa
The truck crosses the Atlantic. We land in West Africa and begin driving north — Ghana, Senegal, Morocco. The continent opens itself to us in extraordinary ways.
2023–2024
The Middle East
Egypt, Jordan, Saudi Arabia, Oman, UAE. Fire at the pyramids. Yoga in Petra. Workshops across the Arabian Peninsula. Ancient worlds meet a living mission.
2024–Present
Turkey, Greece & Albania
Eastern Europe opens a new chapter. Currently in Albania — our 30th country — continuing to teach, perform, and build the worldwide family.
The Harts

Meet Jesse & Catherine

Jesse Hart

Fire Artist · Yoga & Mentorship · Visionary

Jesse has been exploring the intersection of physical mastery, consciousness, and lifestyle design since 2012. He is the founder of Conscious Fitness and True Yoga — a system blending yogic philosophy, movement science, mindset, and manifestation.

With 13+ years of teaching and 175+ five-star client reviews, Jesse works with a small number of private mentorship clients at any given time. His philosophy: the best version of you is already real — we're just closing the gap.

Catherine Hart

Yoga Teacher · Fire Artist · Travel Blogger · Creator

Catherine is a yoga teacher, fire performer, belly dancer, and the heart of Full Circle Phenomenal's creative and community presence. She co-teaches, co-performs, and co-authors everything this brand puts into the world.

As a mother, teacher, and creator living fully on the road, Catherine brings grounded feminine wisdom and adventurous spirit to every offering — from workshops in jungle retreats to flow arts on ancient ruins.

The Youngest Nomad

Prana

"She has never been to our home country, for each country has become home in a way."

Born in Ecuador in 2021, Prana is our daughter and the youngest global citizen we know. She has crossed oceans on ships, driven through deserts, danced at festivals in West Africa, and played in the ancient world of the Middle East.

She is the living proof of what this journey is really about — that children can grow up in the whole world as their classroom, that home is a feeling you carry, not a place you're from.
