Our Story

From Oregon to the World

We are Jesse and Catherine Hart — soul partners, performers, trainers, and nomadic visionaries. In 2019 we drove a camper truck out of Oregon and haven't stopped moving since.

Through Central and South America (where our daughter Prana was born in Ecuador), shipped to Africa, driven up through the continent, across the Middle East, and now in Eastern Europe — 30 countries and counting.

We are the first ever whole-world touring team of yoga teachers and fire performers. This is not a trip. It's a life.
